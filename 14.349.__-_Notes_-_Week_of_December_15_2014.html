Exploring reinforcing selection - hybrid unfitness

Reinforcing Selection

  • females choose non-heterospecifics because of lower hybrid fitness
  • comparing sympatric vs allopatric
    • you expect more sexual distinction in sympatric vs allopatric populations.
  • Limnetic-like and allopatric-like species that are allopatric.

Avida experiments

  • Evolve formerly separate populations together for a while, and then see if they enforce choosyness more strongly after that rather than less.
  • you have to enforce a lot lower fitness of hybrids.
    • Intrinsic hybrid unfitness
      • hybrid environmental unfitness
        • not so much evidence of this having a huge impact in plants, whose chromosomes evolve in length.
        • intrinsic unfitness of hybrid genomes
          • reduced fecundity
          • reduced viability
    • hybrid mating unfitness (you produce a trait that nobody looks at)
    • extrinsic hybrid environmental unfitness
      • You just don't do so well in the environment, even though your genomes work.
    • Look at both components of hybrid unfitness , compare contribution of intrinsic vs extrinsic factors
